# GTM Positioning — FinOps Intelligence AI

## Category Definition

**FinOps Intelligence AI** is an **AI-native finance operations intelligence platform**.

Alternative category names considered:
- AI-powered FinOps platform
- Finance operations intelligence layer
- Proactive financial risk monitoring platform
- AI CFO co-pilot

The chosen category — "AI-native finance operations intelligence platform" — emphasizes three things: the AI-first architecture (not AI bolted onto legacy tools), the finance operations focus (not generic BI), and the intelligence positioning (insights and recommendations, not just data).

---

## Differentiation from Traditional Finance Tools

| Dimension | Traditional BI / ERP Dashboard | FinOps Intelligence AI |
|---|---|---|
| Time orientation | Backward-looking (what happened) | Forward-looking (what will happen) |
| Data sources | Single system (ERP or cloud) | Cross-system (ERP + CRM + cloud + card) |
| Output | Data and charts | Insights, root cause, and recommendations |
| AI role | None or basic anomaly alerts | Continuous multi-agent monitoring |
| Recommendations | None | Prioritized, owner-assigned, impact-estimated |
| Human workflow | Manual analysis required | Human approval, then automated action |
| Report generation | Manual compilation | One-click from live data |
| Response time | Days to weeks | Minutes |

## Messaging Pillars

**1. Stop discovering financial problems at month-end.**  
Finance teams that use FinOps Intelligence AI find budget risks, vendor surprises, and margin compression in week one — not week four.

**2. AI that explains itself.**  
Every insight includes root cause, financial impact, confidence score, and recommended action. No black box. No analyst required to interpret.

**3. Human in the loop — always.**  
FinOps Intelligence AI recommends. Finance leaders decide. Every action requires explicit approval before execution. This is how it should be.

**4. From data to CFO brief in 30 seconds.**  
The platform synthesizes signals across agents, drafts a CFO-ready brief, and delivers it after human review. What used to take 6 analyst hours takes 30 seconds.

**5. Built for finance, not IT.**  
No data engineering required. Connect your ERP, cloud billing, and Salesforce in minutes. Results on day one.

3. **Speed to insight:** Our agents run every 15 minutes. Competitors require manual report runs or end-of-day data refreshes.

## Go-to-Market Motion

**Land:** Lead with Budget Risk Forecasting — the most acute pain point. Every CFO has been surprised by a budget overrun. Demo the budget risk forecast page and the 6-step demo scenario. Get to a pilot in <30 days.

**Expand:** Once budget risk is live and delivering value, expand to Margin Intelligence (CS/RevOps champion), then Vendor Optimization (Procurement champion). Each expansion adds a new buyer and increases platform stickiness.

**Retain:** Weekly CFO brief becomes habitual. Finance teams that use the brief daily do not churn. Measure time-to-habit (< 14 days) as a leading retention indicator.

**Referral:** Finance leaders at SaaS companies know each other. Referral program targeting Finance leaders at portfolio companies of existing customers' VC investors.
